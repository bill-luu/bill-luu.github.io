A Few Tips To Improve Your Builds

  1. Panel Line Your Gundams

    2. Panel lining is the process of drawing fine lines around the detailing of the model parts. You can purchase fine-tip markers to highlight the subtle details of your Gundam.

  2. Removing Your Nubs

    3. When removing parts from the runner, leave a small chunk on the part. Afterwards, use a clipper or hobby knife to remove it completely. Lastly, use 600+ grit sandpaper to rub any stress marks off. The nubs will then be completely gone!
